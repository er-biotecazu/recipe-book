# Pancakes


## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 200 ml milk
- sugar
- salt
- 200 g flour.
- 60 ml soda water
- oil


## Instructions

- mix eggs with milk, a pinch of sugar, a pinch of salt, flour, and soda water
- heat a pan with a bit of oil
- pour enough batter to create a thin pancake and bake for 1-2 minutes on each side 
- enjoy!
